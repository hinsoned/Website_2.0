About Me

My Journey

I spent my early career as a rock musician and record producer, creating music that moved people and shaped experiences. After years in the music industry, I decided to pursue a new passion and went back to school at Columbia University in my thirties.

Graduating with a Computer Science degree, I've discovered that the same creativity and problem-solving skills that made me successful in music now drive my passion for coding. I love building applications that solve real-world problems and create meaningful user experiences.

Today, I combine my artistic background with technical expertise to create innovative software solutions. Whether it's developing web applications, working with audio processing algorithms, or building user interfaces, I bring a unique perspective to every project.
